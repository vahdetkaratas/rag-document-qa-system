Pricing Rules Guide

1. Standard Plans
- Basic: $29/month, 1 user, 5 projects.
- Pro: $79/month, 5 users, 20 projects.
- Enterprise: Custom pricing, unlimited users and projects.

2. Discounts
- Annual billing: 2 months free (pay for 10, get 12).
- Non-profit: 20% discount with valid proof.

3. Add-ons
- Extra users: $15/user/month on Pro.
- Extra storage: $5/10 GB/month.

4. Price Changes
We give 30 days notice for any price increase. You may cancel before the change takes effect without penalty.
