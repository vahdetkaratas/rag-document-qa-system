Refund and Cancellation Policy

1. Cancellation
- Written notice required at least 14 days before the next renewal date.
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
- No partial refunds for unused time in the current period.

2. Refunds
- Full refund if cancellation request is received within 14 days of initial purchase.
- After 14 days, no refund for the current period. Prepaid future periods may be refunded at 50% if more than 30 days remain.

3. Exceptions
- Service failures lasting more than 72 hours may qualify for credit or refund. Open a ticket with support.

4. How to Cancel
Send an email to billing@company.com with subject "Cancellation" and your account ID. You will receive confirmation within 2 business days.
