Technical Support Workflow

1. Ticket Creation
All support requests must be logged as tickets. Include: customer ID, description, severity, and steps to reproduce.

2. Triage
- First response within 4 business hours for standard tickets.
- P1/P2 tickets follow the incident response manual.

3. Resolution
- Use the knowledge base and internal FAQ before escalating.
- Document the solution in the ticket for future reference.
- Close the ticket only after customer confirmation.

4. Escalation
If the issue is not resolved within 2 business days, escalate to Tier 2. See escalation_matrix.pdf.
