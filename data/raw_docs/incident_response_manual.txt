Incident Response Manual

1. Definition
An incident is any unplanned event that causes or may cause disruption to services.

2. Severity Levels
- P1: Full outage, multiple customers affected. Response within 15 minutes.
- P2: Significant degradation. Response within 1 hour.
- P3: Minor impact. Response within 4 hours.
- P4: Low impact. Response within 24 hours.

3. Response Steps
1) Detect and log the incident.
2) Assign severity and notify the on-call engineer.
3) Communicate status to stakeholders every 30 minutes for P1/P2.
4) Resolve and document root cause within 5 business days for P1/P2.

4. Escalation
If not resolved within SLA, escalate to the next level. See escalation_matrix.pdf for contacts.
