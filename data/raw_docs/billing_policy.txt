Billing Policy

1. Billing Cycle
All plans are billed in advance monthly or annually. The billing date is the same as the signup date.

2. Payment Methods
We accept credit card and ACH. Invoice payment is available for Enterprise plans with net-30 terms.

3. Failed Payments
We retry failed payments after 3 and 7 days. After 3 failures, the account is suspended. Restore within 14 days to avoid data deletion.

4. Invoices
Invoices are available in the billing portal. For duplicate or corrected invoices, contact billing@company.com within 30 days.
