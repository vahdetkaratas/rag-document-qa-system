Service Level Policy

1. Uptime
We target 99.9% uptime for the Pro and Enterprise plans. Basic plan target is 99.5%.

2. Support Response
- Pro and Enterprise: 4-hour first response for standard tickets.
- Basic: 24-hour first response.
- P1 incidents: 15-minute response for all plans.

3. Credits
If we fail to meet the uptime SLA in a calendar month, you may request a service credit. Pro: 10% credit for 99.0–99.9% uptime, 25% below 99.0%. Enterprise: negotiated in contract.

4. Exclusions
Scheduled maintenance (announced 7 days in advance) and issues caused by customer actions are excluded from SLA calculations.
