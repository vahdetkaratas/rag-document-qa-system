Customer Onboarding Policy

1. Scope
This policy applies to all new customers signing up for our services.

2. Onboarding Steps
- Account creation within 24 hours of signup.
- Welcome email with login credentials and quick start guide.
- Mandatory orientation call within the first 5 business days.
- First success check at 30 days.

3. Cancellation During Onboarding
Customers may cancel within the first 14 days for a full refund. After 14 days, standard cancellation policy applies.

4. Contact
For onboarding support, contact onboarding@company.com.
